Why would you choose INTELIDENT Dental Clinic?


 

The first and main reason for dental tourism in Bucharest, Romania is to get the same quality of service like in your home country, but at much lower cost. We provide high quality dental care that are at least 50% or more lower prices than Central, Western and Northern Europe countries, UK or North America. We use modern technology, state of the art techniques and equipment and offer warranty on all materials and dental work.

 

Just for example, let's compare prices for implant dentistry and dental implants.

Price of a dental implant in the U.S. range from 1250 $ - 3000 $

Price of a dental implant in Italy range from 1000-2000 Euro

Price of a dental implant in UK is around 1500 – 2000 Euro

Price of dental implant in Romania is about 500 Euro or 650 - 700 $ 

 

As for the quality our dental clinic represents, we can proudly claim that it is one of Romania's most modern dental clinics, where our patients can enjoy all the pleasures of an exclusively cosy environment.
