Dental Implants

  • Smile for less! Straumann Implants

  • Smile for less! Bio Horizons Implants

 

Smile for less! We offer you very affordable dental packages that will save you a lot of money.

 

Want a custom-made package? Our patient center can help you to plan a memorable experience in Bucharest/Romania.

 

 

Full mouth restoration with dental implants and fixed bridges

 

By placing 5 to 8 dental implants on either upper or lower dental arch, a fixed bridge of 9 to 14 teeth can be placed over the implants on top and bottom jaw to restore all of your missing teeth.

 

5 to 8 dental implants for the upper/lower jaw

Upper/lower fixed bridge of 9 to 14 units (teeth)

Panoramic x-rays

Removal of existing crowns if applicable

Crown-root device removal if applicable

Medication kit (pain medication, antibiotics and local anesthesia)

Temporaries

Occlusal guard

 

Free Private transfers Airport/Hotel/Airport*

Free Private transfers Hotel/Clinic/Hotel (only if stay in one of our suggested accomodations)*

*Will apply for any dental treatment over 3400 euro.

 

 

 

The offer includes 6+1 Straumann Implants and up to 13 crowns (teeth) bridge

 

  1. First phase : STRAUMANN Implants 6+1 (you pay 6 and 1 is free) total 7 implants for 4200 euro

Price does not Include abutment for implant.

 

 

After 4-6 months the abutments and crowns are cemented

 

  1. Second phase – after 4-6 months :

 

- Abutment on each implant, total 7 abutments for 1190 euro
