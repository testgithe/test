Why Dental Tourism in Romania?

Dental tourism is one of the most renowned forms of medical tourism and Romania is becoming one of the most popular destinations in this sector. INTELIDENT team formed of highly specialized dentists is prepared to provide our foreign patients premium services and dental care.

At INTELIDENT, our doctors achieve the highest levels in their specialties by attending the best courses in Romania and abroad.
