Dr. Gianina Necula

Dentist - Dental Prosthetics and Esthetics

While a student in the Faculty of Dentistry at the "Carol Davila" University of Medicine and Pharmacy, Dr. Gianina Necula has been a chief assistant at a dental clinic. Her interest for dental care and new ways of dental treatment have determined her to constantly participate in several training courses and congresses:

  • Course for Dental Esthetics by 3M ESPE - Direct technics for morphology's restoration in the  posterior and anterior areas
  • Endodontics Workshop – “Obturation techniques - Cold lateral and warm vertical condensation”
  • Dental prosthetics course – digital evaluation using the colour guide Vita 3D Master
  • Esthetic restorations in the posterior and anterior areas - courses by Ivoclar and GC
  • Teeth preparation for indirect dental veneers
  • Hyaluronica courses - injecting Hyaluronic acid for esthetics results 

In her professional life, as for the personal one, she is guided by her prefered motto: ’As long as you have ambition, perseverence, pacience and willing to work you can achieve any dream’.  And indeed, who would imagine that a prosthetics specialist doctor took part at several Christmas carols concerts all over Romania, all part of a carols album now which makes her proud everyday?
